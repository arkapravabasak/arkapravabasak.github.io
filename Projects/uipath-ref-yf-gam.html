UiPath REFramework – Yahoo Finance Annual Metrics

Project/Use-Case Description

The D'Auro FinTech is an AMC managing portfolios of High Net-worth Individuals by investing their money across multiple portfolios consisting of stocks listed in NASDAQ. The fund management team needs to track the price change as well as historic highs and lows throughout the last 1 year to effectively decide on which stock to purchase or sell.

This automation solution uses the UiPath Robotic Enterprise Framework to extract stock data from Yahoo Finance and calculate annual financial metrics.

The solution demonstrates a scalable enterprise automation architecture with robust error handling and retry mechanisms.

High-level process steps

  1. Read the input excel file containing the Stock Ticker list
  2. Open Yahoo Finance in the browser
  3. Search the Company Stock based on it's Ticker
  4. Navigate to the Historical Data Tab for the Stock
  5. Download the 1Year price data
  6. Calculate: Current Price, Average Price across 1 Year, Dates of Highest and Lowest price with date
  7. Save the details in the output excel file
  8. Save the output file with a corresponding date and time stamp
